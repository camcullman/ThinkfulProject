Where should you ski this weekend?

Find more powder, drive less far

To help you choose where to ski this weekend, this app calculates the top 5 options with the most forecasted powder for the least amount of drive time. When you click "See the best mountains" the app calcualtes a "Trip Appeal Score" (a term I made up that equals snowfall divided by drive-time) for each of the largest 19 mountains in the Northeast . Drive time is determined by average drive time from NYC to the mountain. Forecasted snowfall shows snowfall expected in the next 3 days (so you can plan for the weekend).

In future versions we hope to include mountains outside the Northeast, use your current location to calculate drive time, and add average liftline wait times.

Powered by World Weather Online
